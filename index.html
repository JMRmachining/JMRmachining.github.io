About Us

With over 14 years of experience in the aerospace, defense, and medical industries, JMR Machining has built a reputation for precision, reliability, and efficiency. We specialize in high-quality machining solutions tailored to meet the demands of various industries where precision and durability are critical.

Our expertise extends across a wide range of materials, including aluminum, steel, ABS, Delrin, Inconel, HDPE, LDPE, super alloys, brass, and copper. Whether it's complex aerospace components, durable medical equipment, or high-performance industrial parts, we have the capabilities to deliver with accuracy and consistency.

At JMR Machining, we understand that deadlines are just as important as quality. Our objective is to produce top-tier parts that not only meet but exceed our customers' expectations, all while adhering to their timelines. We leverage advanced machining technologies and meticulous quality control processes to ensure each project is Just Made Right.

Services

Lathe Machining

Our lathe machining services deliver precision turning and cutting of metal components using state-of-the-art lathes. We handle both small-scale and large industrial parts, ensuring tight tolerances and impeccable surface finishes.

Mill Machining

Our mill machining operations are designed for high accuracy and complex geometries. Utilizing advanced milling machines, we produce intricate parts and precise features from a variety of materials, meeting the most demanding specifications.

Mold Making

We specialize in custom mold fabrication with an emphasis on durability and repeatability. Our expert team designs and builds molds tailored to your production needs, ensuring consistent quality and long tool life.

Molding and Casting

Our comprehensive molding and casting services cover a range of techniques to produce high-quality components. We optimize our processes for efficiency and precision, ensuring reliable outcomes for both prototyping and full-scale production.

Composites

Leveraging advanced techniques, our composite services work with modern composite materials to produce lightweight yet robust parts. Ideal for aerospace, defense, and other high-performance industries, our composites meet rigorous strength and durability standards.

3D Printing

Utilizing cutting-edge 3D printing technology, we create detailed prototypes and production parts with rapid turnaround times. Our process supports complex designs and customized components, ensuring high resolution and precision.

Sand Blasting

Our sand blasting service prepares surfaces by cleaning, etching, and smoothing. We efficiently remove rust, paint, and imperfections, creating an ideal base for painting, coating, or further processing.

Polishing

We offer expert polishing services to achieve flawless surface finishes on a variety of materials. Our careful polishing processes enhance both the aesthetic appeal and the functional performance of your components.
